Open-Source Tools for Data Processing

To answer these questions, I primarily rely on quantitative methods with large-scale longitudinal and population-based survey data, including the Panel Study of Income Dynamics (PSID), General Social Survey (GSS) and micro-level Decennial Census data in the United States, as well as the China Family Panel Studies (CFPS) and China Kadoorie Biobank (CKB) in the context of China. I also passionate about developing open-source tools that facilitate processing these complex dataset. You can find some of my ongoing projects and tools I am maintaining on my GitHub page.
